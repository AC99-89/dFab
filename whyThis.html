Why Make This Portfolio?

Originally, this portfolio was made for the PS-70/PHYS S-12/Introduction To Digital Fabrication Class at Harvard University. After the class, I loved doing what I did and I kept on missing my time at the "Fab" Lab; so, I decided to continue after the course ended.

What Is My Goal With This Portfolio?

My goal with this portfolio is to continuously build projects to learn new skills which I can apply to my dream job, Mechatronics Engineering. I plan on working on software, mechanical, electrical, and computer based projects; although, I might throw in a few unique projects to spice things up.
