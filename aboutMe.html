Welcome!

Who am I?

Hello friends! My name is Ali Cicek and I am from Izmir Turkey. I am a music lover; I play the piano, cello, and guitar, and clarinet, but the piano is my main instrument. I have been playing the piano for 8 years and have been constantly improving my technique. My favorite piano piece to play is Toccata and Fugue in D minor BMV 565 by Johann Sebastian Bach.



Aside from music, I also enjoy playing video games. I run a small gaming YouTube channel where I post Minecraft tutorials to make people's lives playing Minecraft easier. I usually only play Minecraft, but when I want a different adventure, I play Terraria and ARK: Survival Evolved.



I also enjoy exercising, reading, occasionally making origami models (My dream origami model to make is a super detailed origami dragon), and drawing. I'm no Van-Gogh, but I could be considered a Piccasso!
