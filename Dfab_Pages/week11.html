This week, we learned various topics. First, we learned how to make better videos using Premier Pro. I have no interest in Premier Pro so I will be using my favorite video editor, Video Shop. We also learned how to vacuum form and I made my own vacuum formation!

To form a vacuum, first cut out a sheet of plastic the size of the vacuum bed and place it on the vacuum bed. Then, turn on the heater and rotate it on the bed and level the bed towards the heater. Wait for the plastic to start drooping, then turn off the heat and rotate it away; immediately pull down the vacuum bed and turn on the vacuum. Wait a few seconds and then turn off the vacuum and you have your own vacuum formation!
